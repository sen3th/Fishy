# Fishy
Charlie was separated from his family while they were out of the anemone. While he was looking for food, a group of hungry sharks tried to eat them. The family got back to the anemone safely, but Charlie didn’t make it home. Now he’s being hunted by the terrifying to the depths of the sea. Then he found the gold. Oh his precious gold. Now he chases the gold for eternity till he meets the relieving death, where he can escape the misery. Or could he? 

## Build
Install Gamemaker Studio and open the project file `Fishy.yyp` in the root of the repository.
